Del a Luna Hotel

Everything you need to know about hotel Del a Luna

Hotel del a Luna is one of the best top ranked hotel in the world. It is located in the heart of Monaco, surrounded by magnificent white sandy beaches

History of the hotel

The hotel was first started by Chef Antonio Ramarez as a resort in 2012 and later on it was transformed to a hotel by a Alexander Xavier in 2018.
Since then, the hotel has grown to be one of the wealthiest hotel in the universe, providing its guest with multitudes of luxurious activities such as:
  • golfing
  • swimming
  • zip linning

Hotel Del a Luna is most certainly the hotel to visit while staying Monaco
